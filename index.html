WHAT WE DO

INCOME TAX

We provide comprehensive income tax services for individuals, professionals, and businesses. From filing your Income Tax Returns (ITR) to tax planning and compliance, our expert team ensures accuracy, timely filing, and maximum savings. Our services include ITR filing, TDS returns, tax consultation, and assistance with notices from the Income Tax Department.

GST

We offer complete GST services to help businesses stay compliant and stress-free. From GST registration to filing monthly, quarterly, and annual returns, our experts handle everything for you. We assist with input tax credit claims, GST audits, and resolving notices from the GST Department. Whether you’re a small business, startup, or large enterprise, we ensure timely and accurate GST compliance to avoid penalties.

MCA

We provide end-to-end services related to the Ministry of Corporate Affairs (MCA) for businesses and companies. Our offerings include company registration (Private Limited, LLP, OPC), annual ROC filings, Director KYC, compliance management, and other MCA-related filings. With our expert guidance, you can stay compliant with all MCA regulations and focus on growing your business.

PAN AND TAN

We assist individuals and businesses with PAN (Permanent Account Number) and TAN (Tax Deduction and Collection Account Number) services. Our team helps you apply for new PAN/TAN, make corrections or updates, and track the status of your application. We ensure a smooth, hassle-free process with quick approvals and accurate documentation.

REGISTRATIONS AND LINCENCES

We help businesses and professionals get all the necessary registrations and licences to operate legally and smoothly. Our services include FSSAI Registration (Food Licence), Shop & Establishment Registration, MSME/Udyam Registration, Import Export Code (IEC), Professional Tax Registration, and more. With expert support and hassle-free processing, we ensure you meet all compliance requirements on time.

CA SERVICES

We provide a wide range of Chartered Accountant (CA) services to support individuals, startups, and businesses. Our offerings include accounting, auditing, tax planning, financial advisory, business compliance, and representation before tax authorities. With expert guidance from qualified professionals, we ensure your finances and compliance are handled efficiently and accurately.
